Mariusz Kiepura (b. 1995, Poland) is a visual artist whose work is based on dynamic range of moods and movements, naturally carved setups of nature and urban life, discretion of temporary and constant reality. Working in the mediums of digital, analog and archival photography, Kiepura focuses on the untold quirks in the conversation between colour and composition. He is currently exploring the theme of relationship, mental health and belonging, searching for organic nuances of the world "in between".
